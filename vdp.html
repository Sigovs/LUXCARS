About this car

Description

This Portofino has covered just 20,352 miles — barely run in for a car built to be driven. It is finished in Blue over a Tan leather cabin, and carries the blue brake calipers, sport exhaust and passenger display specified when it was built.

Ferrari’s 3.9L V8 sits behind the front axle and drives the rear wheels through a seven-speed dual-clutch gearbox. The hardtop folds away in fourteen seconds, so it is a coupé on the way to work and a convertible on the way home — without asking you to choose.

Every car we sell goes through a multi-point inspection before it is listed, and comes with our 3-month / 3,000-mile limited warranty. We can arrange nationwide shipping, and you are welcome to have the car inspected by a third party before you commit.
